Attendance and Marks Calculator

Instructions:

  1. Go to http://parent-login.nmamit.in/index.php and login.
  2. Double-click on any text and click "Select All" to select the entire text.
  3. Copy the selected text and paste it into the "Raw Data" section above.
  4. Click on the "Process Data" button to generate the attendance and marks table.
